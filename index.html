About Me

Hello! I am Wenjie Liao. I received my PhD from the Graduate School of Information, Production and Systems (IPS), Waseda University in April 2026. My research focuses on generative AI, deep learning, natural language processing, and multimodal models.

I currently work at the OPPO AI Center and continue as a Research Assistant at Waseda University's IPS.

My current research centers on on-device / edge deployment of large language models and LLM post-training, alongside ongoing interests in multimodal models and hallucination mitigation in vision-language models.
